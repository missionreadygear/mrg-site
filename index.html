About Mission Ready Gear

Founded by Army veteran Marcus Rodriguez in 2018, Mission Ready Gear emerged from a passion for authentic military equipment and a commitment to serving the tactical community in Houston and beyond.

After serving two tours overseas, Marcus recognized the need for a trusted source of genuine military surplus gear. What started as a small operation in a Houston warehouse has grown into one of Texas's most respected tactical equipment suppliers.

Every piece of equipment we sell has been carefully inspected and authenticated. We understand that when your mission depends on your gear, there's no room for compromise.
